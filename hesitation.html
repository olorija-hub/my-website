Cyberbullying Hesitations & Solutions

Hesitation Solutions / Actions
Fear of Retaliation
  • Report anonymously
  • Save evidence (screenshots)
  • Involve trusted adults or friends
Bystander Effect
  • Take personal responsibility
  • Encourage small supportive actions
  • Promote group norms to act against bullying
Uncertainty or Confusion
  • Learn clear definitions of cyberbullying
  • Review examples and scenarios
  • Ask a trusted adult or peer for advice
Lack of Knowledge or Skills
  • Learn how to block, report, or respond safely
  • Practice role-playing responses
  • Use guides, videos, or workshops
Social Pressure
  • Highlight peers who speak up positively
  • Normalize supporting victims
  • Encourage small, safe actions
Privacy Concerns
  • Use anonymous reporting tools
  • Help without revealing personal info
  • Adjust platform privacy settings
Minimization or Normalization
  • Raise awareness about real harm
  • Encourage empathy for victims
  • Show consequences of harmful online behavior
Emotional Distress
  • Learn coping strategies (deep breathing, breaks)
  • Seek support from friends or adults
  • Start with small helpful actions like reporting
