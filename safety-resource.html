Upstander Tools

What is an upstander: Someone who stands up for others and acts against bullying or injustice.

How to be one: Speak up, support, and act responsibly.

What is cyberbullying: the use of technology, such as computers and phones, to harass, threaten, humiliate, or target another person.

What is empathy: to imagine the feelings that someone else is experiencing

What is an ally: Someone who responds to a bullying situation by supporting the person being bullied
